<Aside>If the sound system is not on, see [Turning the Sound System On](/documentation/audio/turning_sound_system_on/) first.</Aside>

## Start Pairing Mode

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Method 1: Audio Page">
    <Steps>
    1. On the wall touchscreen Homepage, press `Audio`.

       ![Control Screen Homepage](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/homepage.png)

    2. Press `Bluetooth Pair`.

       ![Audio Page](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/audio.png)

    3. Wait a few seconds for pairing mode to activate. You should hear a beep from the room speakers when it starts.
    </Steps>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Method 2: Audio Levels">
    <Steps>
    1. On the Audio page, press `Audio Levels`.
    2. On **Select a Preset**, choose `Computer + Bluetooth Inputs`.
    3. Press `Bluetooth Pairing`.
    4. Wait a few seconds for pairing mode to activate. You should hear a beep from the room speakers when it starts.
    </Steps>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Connect Your Device

<Steps>
1. On your phone, tablet, or computer, open Bluetooth settings.
2. Select `DN-200BR` from the list of available devices.
3. Confirm the device shows as connected.
</Steps>

## Route Bluetooth Audio to Speakers

<Steps>
1. On the Audio page, press `Audio Levels`.
2. On **Select a Preset**, choose `Computer + Bluetooth Inputs`.
3. Find the `Bluetooth` channel card.
4. If `Muted` is red, press once to unmute.
5. Start playback and adjust the `Bluetooth` level with `+1%`, `-1%`, or the slider.
6. Use the `Speakers` card (`Master Output`) on the right for overall room volume.
</Steps>

![Computer and Bluetooth Controls](/assets/images/audio/control/computer-bluetooth.png)

<Aside>
Pairing is for playback devices only. If pairing fails, press `Bluetooth Pair` again and retry from your device's Bluetooth settings.
</Aside>
