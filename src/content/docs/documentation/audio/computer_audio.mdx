## Connect Your Computer

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="HDMI (video + audio)">
    Connect your computer via HDMI. This carries both video and audio in one cable — no separate audio cable needed.

    For port locations, see [Input Methods](/documentation/video/inputs/).
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="3.5mm (audio only)">
    Connect a 3.5mm audio cable from your computer's headphone output to the matching audio port at your chosen input location.

    For port locations, see [Input Methods](/documentation/video/inputs/).
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Open Audio Levels

<Steps>
1. On the wall touchscreen Homepage, press `Audio`.

   ![Control Screen Homepage](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/homepage.png)

2. Press `Audio Levels`.

   ![Audio Page](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/audio.png)

3. On the **Select a Preset** screen, press `Computer + Bluetooth Inputs`.

   ![Audio Preset Selection](/assets/images/audio/control/home.png)
</Steps>

## Use the Computer Input Channel

<Steps>
1. Find the `Computer` channel card.
2. If it shows `Muted` in red, press once to unmute.
3. Start playback on your computer.
4. Adjust the level using `+1%`, `-1%`, or the slider.
</Steps>

![Computer and Bluetooth Controls](/assets/images/audio/control/computer-bluetooth.png)

<Aside type="tip">
Press `About Inputs` on-screen for connection reference details, or `Help` for quick control tips.
</Aside>
