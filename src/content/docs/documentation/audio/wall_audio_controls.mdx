## Open Audio Levels

<Steps>
1. On the wall touchscreen Homepage, press `Audio`.

   ![Control Screen Homepage](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/homepage.png)

2. Press `Audio Levels`.

   ![Audio Page](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/audio.png)
</Steps>

## Select a Preset

Choose the preset that matches your audio source:

- **`Pulpit Wired Mics`** — for pulpit microphones
- **`Wireless Mics`** — for wireless and lapel microphones
- **`Computer + Bluetooth Inputs`** — for computer or Bluetooth audio

![Audio Preset Selection](/assets/images/audio/control/home.png)

## Adjust Channel Levels

<Steps>
1. Find the channel card you want to adjust.
2. If a channel shows `Muted` in red, that channel is muted — no audio will pass.
3. Press `Muted` once to unmute.
4. Use `+1%`, `-1%`, or the slider to set the level.
5. Use the `Speakers` card (`Master Output`) on the right for overall room volume.
</Steps>
