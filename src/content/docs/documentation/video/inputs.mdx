This guide explains how to connect devices in Mackey Hall and switch video sources from the wall touchscreen.

## Connecting Devices

<Steps>
1. Locate the input ports on the wall outside the Scout Closet, under the projector screen, and by the sound system/chair closet wall.
2. Connect your device to the matching labeled port.
3. On the touchscreen, select the input that matches the port label you used.
</Steps>

## Switching Inputs on the Touchscreen

<Steps>
1. Go to the wall touchscreen in Mackey Hall.

2. If not on the Homepage or `Video` page, press the `Home` icon in the top-left corner.

3. Press `Video` to open input controls.

   ![Control Screen Homepage](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/homepage.png)

4. Press the input button that matches your connected port. The selected source lights up blue when active.

   ![Video Controls Page 1](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/video_1.png)

5. Use the left/right arrows at the bottom to move between Video pages.

6. On the second `Video` page, use `Freeze` to pause the current image or `Blank` to black out the screens.

   ![Video Controls Page 2](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/video_2.png)
</Steps>

## Available Inputs

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="HDMI">
    Mackey Hall has three HDMI inputs. HDMI carries both video and audio over one cable.

    - **`Church PC HDMI`** — Under the input selector panel with VGA and RCA. Reserved for the Church PC.
    - **`HDMI 1`** — Near the control touchscreen, for personal devices.
    - **`HDMI 2`** — On the wall outside the Scout Closet, under the projector screen, for personal devices.

    #### Church PC HDMI
    ![Church PC HDMI Input](/assets/images/video/inputs/ports_hdmi-churchpc.png)

    #### HDMI 1
    ![HDMI 1 Input](/assets/images/video/inputs/ports_hdmi1.jpeg)

    #### HDMI 2
    ![HDMI 2 Input](/assets/images/video/inputs/ports_hdmi2.jpeg)
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="VGA">
    There are two VGA inputs. VGA requires two cables for full A/V: VGA (video) plus 3.5mm (audio).

    - **`Laptop 1 (VGA)`** — Under the input selector panel with its matching 3.5mm audio port.
    - **`Laptop 2 (VGA)`** — On the wall outside the Scout Closet, under the projector screen, with its matching 3.5mm audio port.

    #### Laptop 1 (VGA)
    ![VGA 1 Input](/assets/images/video/inputs/ports_vga1.png)

    #### Laptop 2 (VGA)
    ![VGA 2 Input](/assets/images/video/inputs/ports_vga2.jpeg)
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="RCA">
    There is one RCA input under the input selector panel.

    - Yellow — video
    - White/Red — left/right audio

    ![RCA Input](/assets/images/video/inputs/ports_RCA.png)
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
