If you need the reverse process, see [Turning Projector / Rear TV On](/documentation/video/turning_projector_on/).

## Power Off Projector

<Steps>
1. Go to the wall touchscreen in Mackey Hall.

2. If not on the Homepage or `Power` page, press the `Home` icon in the top-left corner.

3. Press `Power` to open power controls.

   ![Control Screen Homepage](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/homepage.png)

4. Press `Projector Off`.

   ![Power Menu Screen](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/power_1.png)

5. Allow about 30 seconds for the projector to fully shut down.
</Steps>

## Power Off Rear TV

<Steps>
1. Go to the wall touchscreen in Mackey Hall.

2. If not on the Homepage or `Power` page, press the `Home` icon in the top-left corner.

3. Press `Power` to open power controls.

   ![Control Screen Homepage](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/homepage.png)

4. Press and hold `Rear TV` for a few seconds to power off.

   ![Power Menu Screen](/assets/images/video/control-screen_new/power_1.png)

5. Wait a few seconds for the rear TV to fully power off.
</Steps>
