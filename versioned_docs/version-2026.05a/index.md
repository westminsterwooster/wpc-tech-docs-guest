# WPC Guest Technology Documentation

Use this guide to run Mackey Hall video and audio for a meeting, class, presentation, or event. If you are not sure where to start, follow the quick start checklist first, then open the guide for the specific equipment you need.

## Quick Start
1. Turn on the [sound system](documentation/audio/turning_sound_system_on.md) if you need microphones, computer audio, or Bluetooth audio.
2. Lower the [projector screen](documentation/video/lowering_screen.md) if the projector will be used.
3. Turn on the [projector or rear TV](documentation/video/turning_projector_on.md).
4. Connect your device and select the matching [video input](documentation/video/inputs.md).
5. Use the iPad [audio controls](documentation/audio/wall_audio_controls.md) to choose the right preset and unmute the channels you need.
6. Test both picture and sound before the event begins.

## Choose What You Need
| Need | Start Here |
| --- | --- |
| Show a laptop, Church PC, or other video source | [Computer Input Methods](documentation/video/inputs.md) |
| Lower or raise the projector screen | [Lowering Projector Screen](documentation/video/lowering_screen.md) or [Retracting Projector Screen](documentation/video/retracting_screen.md) |
| Turn the projector or rear TV on or off | [Turning Projector / Rear TV On](documentation/video/turning_projector_on.md) or [Turning Projector / Rear TV Off](documentation/video/turning_projector_off.md) |
| Use microphones | [Connecting and Using Microphones](documentation/audio/microphones.md) |
| Play sound from a computer | [Using Computer Audio](documentation/audio/computer_audio.md) |
| Play sound from a phone, tablet, or laptop over Bluetooth | [Bluetooth Pairing](documentation/audio/bluetooth_pairing.md) |
| Adjust volume, presets, or muted channels | [Using Audio Controls](documentation/audio/wall_audio_controls.md) |

## Shutdown Checklist
1. Turn off the [projector or rear TV](documentation/video/turning_projector_off.md).
2. Retract the [projector screen](documentation/video/retracting_screen.md) if it was lowered.
3. Mute or lower audio channels on the iPad if needed.
4. Disconnect personal devices and return cables, microphones, and the iPad to their normal locations.
